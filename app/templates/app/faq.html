General

Is account registration required?

Account registration at Crumbly is required only if you want to create and manage Crumbs. No account is necessary to play.

For Creators

How do I create a new Crumb?

Follow these steps:

  1. Login or Signup to get access
  2. Click on My Crumbs to view list of crumbs you have created
  3. Click on Create to pull up form for creating new Crumbs

Do I have to set a start and end time for each Crumb?

No, you do not require a start and end time for each crumb. If you leave these fields blank, then your treasure hunt will remain active indefinitely

How do I join a Crumb?

Follow these steps:

  • With a Crumb Code: If you have a Crumb Code, text "crumb " to +1-(201)-730-4915
  • Via email: You might be invited to a Crumb via email. Click the link and follow instructions

How can I find more Crumbs?

Look out for Crumb codes at local retailers, coffee shops. Sometimes we hide them on Craigslists Other times they might be stuck to a telephone post.

We will be releasing a list of publicly avaialble Crumbs shortly!
